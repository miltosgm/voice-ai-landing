Frequently Asked Questions

Does Sofia work for my industry?

Sofia works for B2B outbound in any industry: SaaS, fintech, real estate, services, construction, staffing, and more. If you're selling B2B, Sofia can do it.

What if prospects hang up?

Sofia handles hang-ups gracefully. She logs it, moves to the next prospect, and your team sees the outcome. No wasted time on rejections.

Can I use my own voicemail list?

Yes. Upload CSV, connect your CRM, or paste a list. Sofia works with any prospect list. No setup fees or contracts.

What about compliance and legal?

Sofia complies with all TCPA regulations. We log consent, recordings, and call data. Full audit trails for compliance teams.

How much setup is involved?

Sign up, write your pitch (2 min), upload your list (2 min), hit start. Sofia goes live in under 5 minutes. No dev work needed.

Can I pause or cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no lock-ins. Pause calls, cancel anytime. We just ask for 48-hour notice on cancellations.
