Enterprise FAQ

What's your SLA for enterprise customers? −
We guarantee 99.9% uptime. 24/7 priority support. If we go down, you get service credits. Enterprise plans include dedicated account manager for escalations.
Can you integrate with our custom CRM? +
Yes. We support Salesforce natively. For other CRMs, we have webhook API access and can build custom integrations. Your dedicated account manager handles setup.
How do you handle GDPR/data residency? +
We're GDPR compliant. Data can be stored in US or EU per your requirements. We conduct annual security audits. Full documentation available.
Do you offer white-label options? +
Yes, on Enterprise and Custom plans. You can brand Athena as your own platform. Support and infrastructure remain ours.
What's your implementation timeline? +
Typically 2-4 weeks for full deployment. Includes dedicated onboarding, training, and initial optimization. Faster if using standard integrations.
