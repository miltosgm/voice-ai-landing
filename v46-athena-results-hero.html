Frequently Asked Questions

How many calls can I make in week 1? −
With the Starter plan (1,000 calls/month), most customers make 200-300 calls in week 1. Growth plan users easily hit 1,000+ calls in week 1. The bottleneck is always how fast you can update your pitch, not Athena's calling capacity.
What's the average meeting booking rate? +
150+ meetings per 1,000 calls on average. Some verticals (fintech, SaaS) see higher rates. This depends on your pitch quality and target list, but Athena's qualifying criteria are strict: real meetings only, no time-wasters.
Will prospects know it's AI? +
No. Athena uses premium human-quality voices with natural pacing and inflection. Most prospects never realize it's AI. Our voice quality is significantly better than competitors.
How long does setup take? +
5 minutes. Upload your prospect list (CSV or Salesforce API). Configure your pitch (opener, objection handlers, closing). Athena starts calling immediately.
Do you integrate with Salesforce? +
Yes. Growth and Enterprise plans include automatic Salesforce sync. Qualified leads push to your CRM in real-time with full transcripts and call notes.
What if I'm not happy? +
30-day money-back guarantee. No questions asked. We're confident you'll see results, but if you don't, we refund your entire subscription.
