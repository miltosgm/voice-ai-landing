If you're spending 6 figures on SDRs and getting mediocre results, you're not alone. The problem isn't your team. It's the broken model of manual outbound. Here's how to fix it.

7 Critical Failures of Manual Outbound

📉

Severely Limited Call Volume

Average SDR: 100-200 calls/month. With list management, admin, CRM data entry, they're not actually selling 60% of the day.

😴

Pitch Consistency Collapses

Pitch quality depends on mood, energy level, time of day. Monday calls are different from Friday calls. Same script, wildly different effectiveness.

💸

Cost is Unsustainable

$65K salary + $25K overhead + taxes = $130K minimum per SDR. To 3x pipeline, hire 3 more SDRs. You're now $390K deeper.

🔄

Turnover Destroys Momentum

SDRs burn out in 12-18 months. Each departure costs 8-12 weeks of ramp time for replacement. You're constantly rebuilding.

📱

Time-Zone Limited Calling

SDRs work 9-5 in one time zone. Prospects sleep for half your selling day. You miss Eastern morning calls if you're West Coast.

⏰

Objections Kill Forward Momentum

Prospect says "I'm interested but it's not the right time." SDR passes to sales. Deal gets stale. You needed real-time objection handling.

🎯

Qualification is Inconsistent

SDR A qualifies aggressively. SDR B qualifies conservatively. Sales team gets 40% junk leads. Nobody wins.
